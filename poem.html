For Sweetu 🌹

I’m not really a poet,

so I don’t know the perfect words to write.

But sometimes the best things are simple,

just like the way you are.


You are one of the kindest people I know.

The way you help others,

the way you care for people who need it,

sometimes it honestly feels like

kindness just naturally lives inside you.


You are a teacher,

but also still a student,

always learning, always growing.

And somehow you still find time

to help everyone around you.


The way you care about kids

is something really special.

You treat them with so much patience and love,

as if they were your own.


And the thing I admire the most

is your ability to forgive.


Whenever you speak,

I just want to keep listening.

It doesn’t matter what the topic is,

your voice makes everything feel calm.


You are intelligent, brave,

and stronger than you probably realize.

Even when life gives you stress

or difficult days,

you still rise again.


And maybe that’s why

you are someone I could never forget.


To me,

you are like my favorite song.

The one I could listen to all night

on repeat

and still never get tired of.


And maybe that’s why

I call you Sweetu.

Because just like chocolate

given to a kid,

your kindness brings

that same simple happiness.
